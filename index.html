Higher Chinese Flashcards

Controls:

  • Chars are parsed from https://www.moe.gov.sg/-/media/files/primary/2015characterlistprimaryhigherchinese.pdf
  • Select a child profile (up to 5) to save and load progress.
  • Use the arrow keys or swipe to navigate.
  • Click the card or press the spacebar to flip it in 'Character Only' mode.

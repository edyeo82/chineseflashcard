# Higher Chinese Word Bank

A companion practice app for the main Chinese flashcard site.

## Current behaviour

- The practice deck is made of character cards from the main Chinese flashcard app.
- Level and Lesson follow the main app's structure.
- Helper words, phrases and sentences are shown on the reveal side of a character card.
- Helper examples are not separate swipe cards.
- Helper examples unlock only when all their Chinese characters have already appeared in the main list up to the selected lesson.
- Known-character progress is saved separately for each child on the current browser/device.

## Example

For a character such as 鱼, reveal shows its pinyin and helper examples such as 雨衣 / 一只鱼 when those helper examples are unlocked.

## Controls

- Tap centre of card: reveal / hide pinyin and helper examples.
- Tap left/right side or arrows: previous / next.
- Swipe right: know and remove from active practice.
- Swipe left: don't know and move on.
- Use Known characters to restore any character marked known by mistake.
